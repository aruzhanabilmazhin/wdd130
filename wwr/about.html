About Wandy Rafting Company

Founded by Aruzhan Abilmazhin, a passionate nature enthusiast, in 2019. We've established a reputation for unforgettable white-water rafting experiences. We offer a variety of trips, including full moon rafting adventures, to cater to all interests. With a team of experienced river guides dedicated to safety and fun, we ensure a thrilling adventure for all guests. We're passionate about sharing the love of white-water rafting and the beauty of nature with future generations.
